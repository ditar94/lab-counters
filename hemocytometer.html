Hemocytometer Counter


Cell counter for tallying the amount of red blood cells and white blood cells in a body fluid.

Instructions:
1. Click the area that says "Click here to count" and use the 1 and 3 keys to count RBCs and TNCs, respectively.
OR
Click/Tap the corresponding buttons to count.
(The "Add" button displays the current mode and can be toggled to "Subtract")
2. Click "Done" on both sides to see final RBC and TNC calculations
